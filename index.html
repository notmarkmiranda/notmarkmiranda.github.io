About Me

I'm an alumni from the Turing School of Software & Design. I started here on February 1, 2016 and graduated on August 5, 2016. I am focused on Ruby on Rails, Javascript, JQuery, AJAX, ActiveRecord, TDD (RSpec and TestUnit), paired programming, git/github, and various other developery things.

I'm a creative thinking full-stack developer with a thirst to learn new and efficient ways to reach a solution. I'm a deep-thinking problem solver that believes heavily that test driven development can truly save time in the lifetime of creating a new, or expanding an existing product. I like to use software to build bridges between people and the places they're trying to get while solving and taking note of all of the unforeseen problems that may arise while said bridge is being constructed.

In my spare time, I tend to work on side projects that you can find listed below. I especially like to spend time with my wife, Holly, and our dogs, Minnow and Barrington, ride my bike around town, hang out with friends, and solve problems that will hopefully one day change the world.

Please feel free to reach out to me if you have any questions, comments or concerns.
